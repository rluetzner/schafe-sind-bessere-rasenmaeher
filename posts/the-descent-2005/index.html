I always get this mixed up with As Above, So Below, mostly because I know that both movies take place in caves. Whereas I had seen As Above, So Below before, The Descent was a new experience for me.

The film tells the story of an all female group of friends that go caving together. During the trip they find out that they are not as close knit friends as they thought they were.
